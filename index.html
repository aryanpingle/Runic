RUNIC

This is a fan-made website that helps you read the in-game language of Tunic - what the community calls, "Runic".

Runic translator

Anything you type in English will be translated to phonetic, and then converted to Runic.
Put phrases within '@' to display them in English, like '@The Librarian@'

Rune Vowel Table

Click on any card to copy the vowel.

Rune Consonant Table

Click on any card to copy the consonant.

Runic Playground

Create your own runes by clicking on segments.
