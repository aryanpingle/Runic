RUNIC

This is a fan made website that helps you read the in-game language of Tunic - what the community calls, Runic.

Convert English text to phonetic - and then to a Runic image.
Put phrases within @ to display them in English, like @The Librarian@.

The perimeter of a rune describes the vowel - click on a card to copy it!

The inner segments of a rune describes the consonant - click on a card to copy it!

Create your own runes by clicking on segments.

See what fans are saying about this site!
